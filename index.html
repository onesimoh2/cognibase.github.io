Truthfulness must be a natural condition of Large Language Models (LLM).

To achieve this, we aim to create a human-curated, unbiased, and non-opiniated knowledge base of facts accessible to anyone, anywhere. This knowledge base will be structured by themes and topics and will be extended systematically.

Currently, non-truthful responses in LLM are often addressed by accessing multiple websites for additional information, which sometime leads to incomplete and biased results. This is why human revision is necessary.

We plan to realize our ambitious goal by using cutting-edge natural language processing technology. Autonomous agents powered by GPT-4 will create the database of facts from the internet and guide the human revision, checking on biases and opiniated texts and will monitor the sources, for newly created knowledge.

Our revenues will come from subscriptions to our chat platform, advertising for those free subscribers, pay-per-use of our cloud-based API and leasing our ever-growing knowledge base to third-party organizations. With our diverse streams of revenue, we can ensure rapid growth and provide measurable advances month after month.

The need for reliable information will grow exponentially as LLM becomes more widely used and CogniBase is poised to become the go-to platform for expanding knowledge and accelerating learning. Join us today and be part of the future of cognitive enhancement powered by truthful AI!
